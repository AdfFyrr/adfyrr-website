FAQs

Questions Indian founders
ask us most.

What happens after I pay the ₹499 booking fee?
You're immediately redirected to our calendar to pick a slot for a 1-hour strategy call on Google Meet. On the call we audit your products, ad account and market, and map a testing plan for your budget. The ₹499 is fully adjusted against your first month's fee if you sign up.
Who pays for the ad spend?
You do — directly to Meta from your own ad account. Our monthly fee is purely for management, testing and strategy. You always have full visibility and ownership of your ad account and data.
Do you create the ad creatives?
Creatives are produced by your team — but you're never guessing. We give you precise creative direction for every test: the hooks, angles and formats that are getting the highest CTR in your niche right now.
What's the minimum ad spend to work with you?
Our plans are built for brands spending at least ₹1,00,000 per month on Meta. If you're below that, book the ₹499 strategy call anyway — we'll tell you honestly whether you're ready and what to fix first.
Is there a lock-in period?
Plans run in rolling 30-day cycles with the first month payable in advance. We recommend a 3-month runway — product testing compounds, and the biggest wins usually come after the first full testing cycle.
Are the prices inclusive of GST?
Prices are exclusive of GST. 18% GST applies on all plans and a proper GST invoice is issued for every payment.
