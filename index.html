FAQ

Asked on almost
every fuel call.

How fast will we see results?
The first 2–3 weeks are structured testing — that's where the winning angles come from. Most brands see meaningful movement in 30–45 days, and the compounding shows in months 2–3. Anyone promising 5x ROAS in week one is selling you a screenshot.
Why "flat retainer or % of spend, whichever is higher"?
It's how serious agencies stay aligned with you. A pure flat fee means we earn the same whether you scale or stall. A pure percentage tempts agencies to push spend you don't need. The industry standard is 10–20% of spend — ours is capped at 6–10% and only kicks in once your spend outgrows the flat fee.
What's the minimum ad budget to work with you?
₹1L/month in ad spend for Ignite. Below that, testing is too slow to learn anything reliable — we'd rather you come back when the budget can actually buy data.
Do you lock us into a long contract?
No. Retainers are month-to-month after an initial 90-day sprint — the honest minimum for creative testing to compound. 92% of clients stay past 6 months because the numbers work, not because a contract says so.
Who actually works on our account?
The people you meet on the fuel call. We keep a small roster precisely so senior strategists run accounts — there is no hand-off to a junior after you sign.
What is the 10-min Fuel Call?
A short, direct call: we look at your numbers, tell you honestly whether we can move them, and show relevant case studies. If we're not the right fit, we'll say so and point you somewhere better.
