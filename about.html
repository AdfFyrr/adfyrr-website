AdFyrr was founded on a simple belief: performance marketing should be accountable to the bottom line. We're the growth partner ambitious brands wish they'd hired sooner.

Our story

Born from a frustration with vanity metrics

AdFyrr started because we were tired of watching great brands get burned by agencies that optimized for impressions, clicks, and screenshots — not profit.

We built a different kind of agency: one staffed by senior operators who think like founders, obsess over unit economics, and treat every advertising dollar as if it were our own. No bloated teams, no junior hand-offs, no smoke and mirrors.

Today, AdFyrr manages profitable ad spend for ecommerce and D2C brands across India, the US and beyond — and we're just getting started.
